How to use the new makefiles

  • Remember, `make help` is your friend
  • make test
  • make run-dgraph CMD_PREFIX=/usr/bin/gdb CMD_ARGS='--args foo'
  • make run-dgraph FLAVOR=gprof (or purify, or valgrind, or quantify)
  • make ../linux-x86_64-noopt-tcmalloc/build/eosi/threading/Condition.o
  • make -j5
  • Will use env vars, but defaults are always right
